Welcome to my final project.

This project is based off of the game "Pong." In order to begin, you must first press the “play” button on the splash screen. Once the game starts up, you can control the left paddle with W, S, A, and D, and the right paddle with the arrow keys. The goal is to reflect the ball every time; if you don’t, and the ball gets through your side, you will receive one point. The reverse is also true. The player with the least amount of points is winning.
